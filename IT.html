Departments Of Information Technology

Primary objective of this programme is to prepare the students for wide range of IT careers equipping them with unique enriching experience of molding tomorrows technocrats with high caliber and commitment. Information Technology laboratories are equipped with state of art laboratories with more than 150 computers in LAN, extensive experiments are conducted on Project Laboratory, Software Engineering Laboratory, Network Engineering Laboratory which is an admirable attribute of the program. Faculty members are dedicated to impart excellence in Information Technology and energetic in research development work to cope with recent market drift. The Information Technology curriculum provides students with a sufficient background in mathematics, Information Theory, Computer Science and Communication Technology to analyze and design complex Information Technology systems.
